Founding Story

Anissa & Tam Co. was established by two fashion enthusiasts, Anissa and Tam, who shared a passion for eco-friendly fashion. They recongized the need for stainable practices in the fashion industry and set out to create a brand that would set new standards.

Eco-friendly clothing

We offer a range of fashion products, including clothing, accessories, and footwear. All products are made from sustainable materials like organic cotton, recycled polyster, and natural dyes. The desgins blend contemporary fashion trends with timeless styles, catering to a broad audience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the fashion industry by offering stylish, sustainable clothing. We envision a world where fashion and sustainablity coexist, reducing the industry's carbon footprint and promoting ethical practices.

What's Next?

We aim to continuously innovate in livable fashion, exploring new materials and techniques. We plan to expand our reach globally while maintaing our core values of local sourcing and enviormental responsibility.
